Frequently Asked Questions

What qualifications are required to join Tez Health?

You must be a certified healthcare professional (Nurse, Doctor, or Physiotherapist) with at least 2–5 years of clinical experience depending on the role.

How much can I earn per month?

Professionals can earn up to ₹50,000 per month, depending on the number of shifts and services completed.

Do I get to choose my working hours?

Yes. Tez Health offers full flexibility — you can choose shifts that fit your schedule, whether part-time or full-time.

Is this a full-time or part-time opportunity?

Both options are available. You can work part-time, full-time, or on flexible duty-based schedules.

Will I receive training before starting?

Yes. Tez Health provides orientation, skill refreshers, and service protocol training for all professionals.

Do I need to bring my own equipment?

Basic medical kits are provided. For specialized services, our operations team ensures you have everything required.

Can I work only in my local area?

Yes. We try to assign cases within your preferred locality for convenience and quicker response times.

Is transportation provided?

You may use your own vehicle. Travel allowances are offered for longer-distance visits.

How do I apply to join Tez Health?

Simply fill out the application form on our website, upload your documents, and our onboarding team will contact you within 24–48 hours.
