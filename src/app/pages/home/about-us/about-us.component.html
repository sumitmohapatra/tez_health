Redefining care at home

Tez Health is reimagining how India accesses everyday healthcare. We are a technology-driven home healthcare network that brings hospital-grade medical care to your doorstep in Minutes. Whether you need an injection, IV therapy, wound care, minor illness treatment, post-surgery support, or monitoring for chronic conditions, Tez Health ensures safe, protocol-driven care at home.

Built by healthcare experts, our platform combines AI-powered dispatch, digital clinical protocols, and real-time quality monitoring to deliver care that matches hospital standards—without the travel, waiting, or high consultation costs. With thousands of families already trusting Tez Health, we are on a mission to make “care at home” the new normal in India.

“In a world of instant deliveries, healthcare should be just as fast. Tez Health ensures families no longer travel or wait endlessly for basic medical care.”
