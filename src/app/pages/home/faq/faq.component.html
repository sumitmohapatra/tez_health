Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I book a home healthcare service?

Most services can be scheduled within 15–30 minutes depending on your location and provider availability.

What types of conditions do you treat at home?

We handle minor injuries, minor illnesses, injections, wound care, diagnostics, and general nursing care — all from your home.

Are your nurses and healthcare providers certified?

Yes. Every professional is licensed, verified, background-checked, and trained for safe in-home medical care.

Do I need a prescription for home nursing services?

Not always. Many services such as vitals check, injections (with prescription), wound care, and IV therapies can be booked directly.

Is there an additional charge for urgent visits?

Urgent or priority visits may have an added fee, but all pricing is shown clearly before you confirm your booking.

What if I need recurring or long-term care?

We offer weekly and monthly care plans for elders, chronic care patients, post-surgery support, and long-term nursing needs.
