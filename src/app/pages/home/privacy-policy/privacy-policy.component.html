Last Updated: 18-Dec-2025

Tez Health (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, process, and protect your personal and medical information when you use our website, mobile application, or services.

By accessing Tez Health or using our services, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

Personal Information

  • Address / location
  • Gender

Medical Information

  • Symptoms and medical complaints
  • Past medical history
  • Current medications
  • Doctor prescriptions
  • Diagnostic reports
  • Treatment notes generated during service

Payment Information

  • UPI ID, wallet details, or partial card information

All payments are processed through secure third-party payment gateways.

Technical & Usage Data

Used to assign the nearest nurse, paramedic, doctor, or ambulance for timely service delivery.

We do not sell or rent your personal data.

All partners are bound by strict confidentiality and data-protection standards.

4. Data Storage & Security

While no system is completely secure, we follow industry best practices.
