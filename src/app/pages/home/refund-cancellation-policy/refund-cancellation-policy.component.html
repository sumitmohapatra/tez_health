Refund & Cancellation Policy

At Tez Health, we strive to deliver timely, reliable, and high-quality healthcare services at your doorstep. This policy ensures fairness while respecting the time of our medical professionals and operational resources.

1. Service Cancellation Policy

Cancellation before professional assignment:
You may cancel your booking at no extra charge if no nurse or doctor has been assigned yet.

Cancellation after assignment but before arrival:
If a nurse or doctor has been assigned but has not yet started traveling, a cancellation fee will be charged.
If the assigned professional has already started traveling, a visit fee will apply.

Cancellation at doorstep or after arrival:
If the service is canceled upon arrival of the nurse or doctor, the full visit charge will be applicable.

Advance bookings for packages or multi-day care

  • Refunds are provided on a pro-rata basis for unused days.
  • Days already attended or canceled at the doorstep are not refundable.

3. Rescheduling Policy

One free reschedule is allowed if requested at least two hours before the appointment time. Repeated rescheduling requests may attract a rescheduling fee.

4. No-Show Policy

If the patient or family member is unavailable or unresponsive at the time of arrival, it will be treated as a no-show and the full visit fee will be charged.

5. Exceptional Situations

We understand emergencies happen. In genuine medical or family emergencies, Tez Health may offer flexibility and waive applicable fees at its discretion.
